============
Requirements
============

Software Platform
=================

This library works with the AMD Vitis |trade| software platform. It inherits the system requirements of the Vitis platform and XRT.

Supported operating systems are:

- RHEL 8.10, 9.6, 9.7, 10.0, and 10.1
- Ubuntu 22.04.5 LTS, 24.04.2 LTS, and 24.04.3 LTS
- AlmaLinux OS 8.10, 9.6, 9.7, 10.0, and 10.1
- Rocky 8.10, 9.6, 9.7, 10.0, and 10.1

Hardware Platform
=================

AI Engine graphs and kernels work with AMD Versal |trade| AI Core, Versal AI Edge, and Versal AI Edge Gen 2 devices,
which are available on the VCK190, VEK280, and VEK385 boards, respectively.
